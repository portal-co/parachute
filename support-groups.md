# Why?

Child Safety. From my observation, children form groups--which may or may not include parents--which use parental control software for safety. For that reason, we support survelliance-less laws (like Sammy's Law is/was) which allow unconditional parental control. Our native solution, though, is directly inspired by this evidence.

Support Groups supply rock-solid child safety without holding any data, much less hoards of it. They rely on real insights from real parental control usage, cryptographic privacy, and a firm anti-survelliance stance.

# How

People join MPC-powered support groups. They can only leave if ALL other members have quorum that can happen. Support groups have parental controls, both legally mandated, and directly observed. Support groups, as mentioned, are MPC-powered; external actors can't see anything from the group (even US), data is homomorphically and broadcastedly encrypted (powerred by [Volar](https://github.com/portal-co/volar)), etc.

Support groups don't train models, they really just shuffle logs (location, screen time) and "vouch requests" (app installation requests, screen time bypass requests, certain features, removal), encrypted, internally.
